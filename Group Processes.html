Group Processes

Throughout assignment 2 the group performed exceptionally. From the first meeting we had already established a rough guideline on what work we want done each week and we were able to keep track of it all through a Trello board. The Trello board made it very easy to access information such as, what needed to be done, when is each part needed to be done, who is doing what, and the meeting times and agendas. The work allocated was flexible so rather than allocating all parts from the first day, we only allocated sections at a time just in case a part took someone longer than expected or other commitments made the section take longer than expected. This proved to be useful, and we believe it reduced stress within the group. Work is set with deadlines being next meeting which assures that each person has content to talk about during the meeting and if they are unable to finish due to an issue they had they are able to bring it up in the meeting to talk about it. We believe this format works well and will continue to use it through assignment 3.
